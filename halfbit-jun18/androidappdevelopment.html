Android is an OS created by Google, in light of an altered variant of the Linux piece and other open source programming and outlined basically for touchscreen cell phones, for example, smartphones and tablets.

The way forward is obviously encouraging for Android application engineers, given the mind boggling development the stage has encountered throughout the most recent couple of years, including ongoing moves into new and excited gadgets. There will be great growth for applications to be built for developing markets, where critical development in Android-based smartphone possession will keep on providing job doors open for app developers. Android is an evergrowing operating system which is used in different platforms other than Smartphones and Tablets like Watches, Home Automation, Car Navigation Systems and even laptops (Chrome OS.)

With over 85%of market share android dominates the global smartphone world. There are over a millon apps and games on play store. With every industry launching their apps, there is a huge requirement of android developers. In this course you will learn everything that is needed to build stunning android apps.
